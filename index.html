DOLL HOUSE DEATH RACE

KILL AND SURVIVE TO ESCAPE THE MANSION! BE THE LAST DOLL STANDING!

HOW TO PLAY:

  1. Enter a name for your doll.
  2. Click the Add button to create a random doll with that name.
  3. Keep adding dolls until you have at least 2.
  4. When you're ready, click Simulate to start the game.
  5. The game will randomly select who kills, who gets killed, and the weapon used.
  6. Keep pressing Simulate until only one doll survives.
  7. Enjoy the game!

Note: When a doll dies, their image will turn greyscale in the doll list.
